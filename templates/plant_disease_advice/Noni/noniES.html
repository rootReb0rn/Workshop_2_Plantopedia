Noni Plant

Disease Advice

Does the plant have black leaf spots and leaf veins or stem blight ?

Are there brown to black soft rot of fruits ?

Does the plant display severe defoliation and death of stem ? (hanging and diseased leaves)

Are there visible light brown color spots surrounded by a conspicuous, diffuse, yellow halos?

Does the plant have visible pathogens on the upper surface and forms a filamentous thallus above the epidermis which moves intercellularly to the lower leaf surface?

Does the plant have large expanding leaf spots with dark to tan centers and diffuse, irregular margins?

Do the lesions expand and the concentric rings become visible in the lesions as the lesions grow each day?

Do the lesions coalesce to form large, blighted areas on leaves, often at leaf margins?

"Does the injured tissue takes on a silvery or bleached appearance and eventually turn bleached and then dark brown color?

Do large lesions develop on leaves, with bleached centers and irregular margins?

Does the plant have yellow, chlorotic areas surrounded or associated with visible lesions?
